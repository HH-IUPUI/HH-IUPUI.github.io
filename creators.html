Behind the curtains!

This website is the subproject of the main collaboration project of two educational institutes: IUPUI and HHUAS. The project has about 10 people from the American university and 10 ppl from Finnish university. The project’s main purpose is to present the learning outcome of basic skills for Web Development. The main website includes smaller websites. The current website focuses on Intercultural communication in a Global Classroom: USA & Finland.

The current website’s creators include two students from USA and two people from Finland. Therefore, the focus is on cultural differences and similarities between American and Finnish cultures.

This is a more fun-based project rather than a theory-based. Please do not take every word seriously. We do not intend to state that these are 100 percent valuable findings on American and Finnish cultures. However, we do believe it is important to get a look at how cultures influence people’s behavior and therefore their activities on international projects.

The project brought us together and we are glad to announce that we have got along with each other while learning Web Development basics.
